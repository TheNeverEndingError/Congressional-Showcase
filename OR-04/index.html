Connecting Generations, Building Community.

By Rylan Pierce | OR-04

About Me

I am a high school junior who has been engineering and programming projects since middle school. I've worked on everything from creating my own video games to building arcade machines and software for my community, always hoping to better the lives of the people around me.

The Goal

Living in a relatively small retirement community, one of the biggest problems I see is a simple lack of connection. Generations Connect is meant to bridge the gap between generations, allowing high schoolers and senior citizens to connect through tasks in order to strengthen our communities.
